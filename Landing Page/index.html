Our Story

This is not just a story but our passion

Little Joy Beadies started from an accident that started from the desire to make accessories that are cheap but still aesthetically beautiful, we want to prove that beautiful accessories do not have to be expensive and difficult to obtain, everyone has the right to use and enjoy them. From there, Little Joy Beadies products in the form of handicrafts come in several product variants such as Bracelets, Necklaces, Keychain, Straphone, Rings and other accessories. What we can guarantee is we Spreading joy and 100% made with love.
